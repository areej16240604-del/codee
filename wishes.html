A Letter For Shayan

Hey Shayan,

Sab se pehle, Happy 22nd Birthday! Allah tumhein hamesha khush rakhe, sehat de, kamyabi de, aur tumhari har jaiz dua aur har acha khwab poora kare. Dua hai ke tumhari zindagi ka yeh naya saal bohat si khushiyan, sukoon aur khoobsurat yaadein lekar aaye.

Sach kahoon to first semester mein tum se dosti hona meri university ki achi yaadon mein se ek hai. Tum hamesha bohat caring, samajhdar aur achay dost rahe ho. Haan, kabhi kabhi tum bohat tang bhi karte ho 😒, lekin phir meri fikr bhi karte ho, mera khayal bhi rakhte ho, aur agar kabhi naraz ho jao to zyada der naraz bhi nahi rehte. Isi liye tumhari yeh baat mujhe hamesha achi lagti hai. Shukriya itne achay dost hone ke liye. Lekin batameez tu ho hi, manatay nhi mujhay bandar gadhay.

Aur haan... aaj tumhein birthday wish kar rahi hoon, warna mera to bilkul bhi irada nahi tha gadhay. 😒 Lekin kya karoon, birthday saal mein sirf ek hi dafa aata hai, is liye socha chalo wish hi kar doon.

Khair... ab tak to tum ne naye dost bhi bana liye honge. Woh bhi tumhein wish karenge next year. Main to chali jao gi. 😌 Tum unhi ke saath khush rehna, khoob enjoy karna, aur apna khayal rakhna.

Aur haan... zyada hero banne ki zarurat nahi hai, samjhay? 😂 Aur agar kabhi mujhe tang kiya na, phir dekh lena. 😌

Bas, Allah se dua hai ke tumhara aaj ka din bohat acha guzre. Dil khol kar enjoy karna, bohat si khushiyan hasil karna, aur hamesha isi tarah hanste muskurate rehna. Allah tumhein hamesha apni hifazat mein rakhe, har qadam par kamyabi de, aur tumhari zindagi ko hamesha khushiyon se bhar de.

Once again, Happy 22nd Birthday Shayan!
Allah tumhein hamesha khush rakhe.
Take care! 🎂
ab ghaib ho jao wapis sy
